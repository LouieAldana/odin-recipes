Burrito

Carne Asada Burrito recipe is simply a warmed tortilla slathered with creamy refried beans and topped with succulent slices of carne asada, jack cheese, buttery avocado, spicy pico de gallo, and rich sour cream (or Mexican crema). Carne Asada Burritos make a seriously satisfying meal and are a hearty and delicious way to feed a hungry group!

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cans refried beans or 2 1/2 cups homemade refried beans, warmed
  • 6 ounces shredded jack cheese 6 ounces or more
  • 2 medium avocados peeled, seeded and thinly sliced
  • sour cream to taste
  • hot sauce such as Tobasco or Cholula, optional

Steps

  1. Prepare Carne Asada according to this recipe.
  2. In a dry pan on the stove top or on a hot grill, warm tortillas about 30 seconds per side. Transfer to platter and cover with foil to keep warm.
  3. To assemble a burrito, spread warm refried beans on the bottom center of a tortilla. Top with 1/6 of the Carne Asada, cheese, avocado slices, Pico de Gallo, sour cream and hot sauce (optional). Fold bottom side of tortilla over toppings and gently pull back to shape filling. Fold up both sides of tortilla toward the center and tightly roll into a cylinder. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
  4. Alternatively, serve warmed tortillas with thinly sliced Carne Asada and individual toppings family-style letting everyone assemble their own burritos.
